# Bigby

**Private, encrypted cloud storage. Built in the UK.**

Bigby is a UK cloud storage service for people and teams who want their files kept private. We are a plain-speaking alternative to Google Drive, Dropbox and OneDrive, built for anyone who has had enough of Big Tech scanning, profiling and monetising their data.

## What we do

- **Encrypted UK storage.** Your files are encrypted at rest on UK servers. We never access, scan or train AI on your content.
- **Real collaboration.** Our Office plans include Collabora, so teams can work on documents, spreadsheets and presentations together without surveillance.
- **Open standards.** WebDAV support comes as standard, because your files should never be locked in.
- **GDPR compliant, UK data residency.** Your data stays in the UK, governed by UK law.

## Why we exist

Most mainstream cloud storage carries a hidden cost. Free tiers are paid for with your data: scanned, indexed, profiled and increasingly fed into AI training. We think storage should work like any honest service. You pay a fair price, we keep your files safe and private, and that is the whole arrangement.

## How we are different

- **You are the customer, not the product.** No free tier, no data harvesting, no adverts.
- **Honest pricing.** Clear tiers from £3.99 per user per month on annual billing. No bloated plans designed to confuse.
- **Independent.** No VC funding and no pressure to squeeze users for growth.
- **Open source stack.** We build on open source software and support open standards.
- **Human support.** Two founders, based in the UK, answering questions ourselves.

## Plans

- **Storage** (files only): 100GB, 500GB or 1TB from £3.99 per user per month
- **Office** (Storage plus Collabora collaboration): from £5.99 per user per month
- **Group** (Office for teams of three or more): from £4.99 per user per month
- **Storage Top-Up**: an extra 100GB for £1.49 per month on any plan

All prices are per user per month on annual billing.
